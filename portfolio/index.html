We are currently designing an AI participant for the 'Um, Actually' web show'. It needs to follow along with the host's reading, detect factual errors in the statements in real time, and provide corrections. Additionally, it must decide whether to trust its own answers. We are also developing a system to automatically evaluate the correctness of both the AI and players' answers. This project mainly focuses on fact checking, large-model enhancement, confidence issues with large models, and sentence matching tasks.
